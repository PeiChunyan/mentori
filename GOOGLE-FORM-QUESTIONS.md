## Form Settings Recommendations

**General Settings:**
- ✅ Collect email addresses
- ✅ Limit to 1 response per person
- ✅ Allow response editing after submit
- ✅ Show progress bar

**Presentation:**
- Use the Mentori brand colors (orange/pink gradient)
- Add your logo
- Include a thank you message at the end

**Confirmation Message:**
```
🎉 Thank you for your feedback!

We're excited to build Mentori Network with insights from people like you. We'll keep you updated on our launch and exclusive early access opportunities.

Meanwhile, feel free to explore the demo at [your-demo-url]

— The Mentori Team
```

**Email Notifications:**
- Set up email notifications to get alerted when someone responds
- Responses will automatically save to Google Sheets

---

## Tips for Better Response Rate

1. Keep the form on one page (not multiple pages)
2. Make questions 3, 4, 9, and 10 optional if getting low responses
3. Test the form yourself before sharing
4. Share the form link on social media with a compelling message
5. Consider offering early access or beta tester perks
